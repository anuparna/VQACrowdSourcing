Instructions

There are four different images in this page.
The question about the image appears on the top and on the left, are the responses collected from 10 different crowd workers.
These responses may or may not overlap.

Every image is followed by six reasons for disagreement among these responses.
We want you to provide us with what you think are the possible reasons for the disagreement.

Here are some examples along with the definition of every reason that we believe might have caused the disagreement.

  • Task Difficulty: The visual questions require domain expertise to answer.
  • Ambiguity: Unclear images or questions leading to different interpretations.
  • Granularity: The resolution, level or scale of the image causing difference in answers.
  • Subjectivity: Opinion related questions can have different answers.
  • Insufficient Visual Evidence: Absence of a sharp and clear image causing different answers.
  • Synonym: All answers capture more or less the same connotation of the visual question.

You may click on the images below and keep your mouse pressed for an enlarged view of the images.


Please follow the steps as mentioned below.

  1. Please read the question and responses and select all the applicable reasons for each of the four images.
  2. You must select atleast one option that describes the reason for disagreement among crowd workers from the given list of reasons.
  3. If you do not agree with any of the given reasons, please select all the closest possible reasons behind the disagreement.
  4. Let us know your thoughts in the comment box at the very bottom of the page. We appreciate your feedback.
